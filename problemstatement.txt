## Project Title

**WorkLink – Hyperlocal Part-Time Job Marketplace**

## Problem Statement

In India, many local employers such as shop owners, households, event organizers, and small businesses frequently need workers for short-term jobs like house shifting, packing, loading/unloading, cleaning, delivery assistance, AC servicing support, and event setup. At the same time, many students, part-time workers, and daily wage laborers are willing to work but struggle to find reliable job opportunities nearby.

Currently, these jobs are mostly shared through word-of-mouth, local brokers, WhatsApp groups, or personal contacts. This creates several problems:

* Employers cannot quickly find available workers.
* Workers miss job opportunities due to limited connections.
* There is no centralized platform for local part-time jobs.
* Communication and hiring processes are unorganized.
* Fake profiles and trust issues make hiring risky.

Therefore, there is a need for a simple digital platform that connects employers and workers within the same locality, allowing employers to post jobs and workers to discover and apply for them easily.

---

## Proposed Solution

WorkLink is a web application that connects local employers and workers through a location-based job marketplace.

The platform contains two types of users:

### 1. Employer

* Creates an account.
* Posts job requirements.
* Specifies location, payment, duration, and number of workers needed.
* Reviews worker applications.
* Selects suitable workers.
* Contacts workers through WhatsApp or phone call.
* Marks jobs as completed.

### 2. Worker

* Creates an account.
* Selects location and skills.
* Sets availability status (Available/Working).
* Views jobs posted in their selected location.
* Applies for jobs.
* Receives notifications when selected.
* Contacts employers through WhatsApp or phone call.

## Simplified Workflow

### Step 1: User Registration

Both employers and workers register using mobile number verification.

↓

### Step 2: Profile Creation

Users complete their profiles and select their location.

↓

### Step 3: Job Posting

Employer posts a job with:

* Job title
* Description
* Payment
* Working hours
* Location

↓

### Step 4: Job Feed

Workers view available jobs in their selected location.

↓

### Step 5: Job Application

Interested workers apply for the job.

↓

### Step 6: Worker Selection

Employer reviews applicants and selects workers.

↓

### Step 7: Notification

Selected workers receive a notification and job status changes to "Booked".

↓

### Step 8: Communication

Employer and worker communicate through:

* WhatsApp
* Phone call

↓

### Step 9: Job Completion

Worker completes the assigned work.

↓

### Step 10: Payment

Employer pays the worker directly through UPI.

↓

### Step 11: Rating & Feedback

Both users can rate each other to improve trust and reliability.

## Key Features

* Mobile OTP Authentication
* Separate Worker and Employer Dashboards
* Job Posting System
* Location-Based Job Feed
* Availability Status
* WhatsApp Integration
* Phone Call Integration
* Job Status Tracking
* User Verification
* Rating and Feedback System
* Real-Time Updates using Firebase

---

## Expected Outcome

The platform will help employers quickly find local workers and provide students and part-time workers with easier access to nearby job opportunities, reducing dependency on brokers and informal hiring methods while improving trust and transparency in local job recruitment.
